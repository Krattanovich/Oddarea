www.waynaclothing.com

Wayna clothing is a company making sustainable fasion for the cold days. Daiane Santos is the founder of the company, with a strong vision a sustainable brand supporting the indigenous people of Peru.


The material used is aplaca wool and the product is hand knitted of indigenous people in Peru. The purpose withe the clothing company is to create a better life for the indigenous people in Peru
